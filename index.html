Top Five: The score is made up of only the Top 5 highest players from a country.

Baseline Subtraction: The 100th player's score is subtracted from everyone else. This creates a balanced upper and lower bracket to work with.

Diminishing Weights: Player 1 adds 100% of their new score, Player 2 adds 50%, Player 3 adds 25%, Player 4 adds 10%, Player 5 adds 5%.
